Contact/About

Hey! My name is Usha Venkat and I am an Illustrator/Graphic Designer in training. I have always been making art and when I finally got the opportunity to pursue it I made sure to make it worth it. I am a maker and every person I've come across describes me as just a whole lot of energy who is always doing something. Creating a company's whole brand and aesthetic is the most powerful opportunity and I'd love to have as many of those as I can get! On a more personal note: I am left handed, love making little clay sculptures, and have a miniature shopping cart which yes...tiny groceries
